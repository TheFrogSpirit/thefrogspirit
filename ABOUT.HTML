The Frog Cult is founded by Battur, aka Batman, a linguist from Mongolia.

Batman is inspired by traditional Mongolian philosophy, morals and beliefs alongside with
buddhism, science/linguistics, classical philosophy, daoism, memes and maybe others.
He enjoys making YouTube videos, playing musical instruments, debating, cracking jokes,
meditating, obsessing over Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Matrix, Star Wars, animes and so on.

He came to this world with the sole purpose of creating the Frog Cult, it has been his
dream even before he was born. The Frog Spirit assigned this role of the holy messenger,
so that other beings would benefit from the tantric secrets of the Frog Cult.
Through good and bad experiences, Batman's vision has been blurred and he strayed away
from the true path. He had forgotten his purpose of life until he was exposed to a great
amount of frog pictures(memes).

He started to feel and remember the long forgotten connection which was chained away deep
in his heart. Batman grabbed a chain of memory, the more he pulled, the more chains resurfaced
back to his mind's surface. After donning the cloth of Froggie, his true self gained back
the control and granted the guidance to Batman - deliver the messages of the Frog Spirit
to all six beings - gods, demi-gods, humans, animals, hungry ghosts and hells.
For they have long forgotten.

The messages of the Frog Spirit will be divided into two main categories - Stories and Practices.
Stories will contain the ancient stories that are no longer on papers with inks. They will
grant clear insight of life and the world. Practices will include methods and approaches to
fix one's own heart, so that the mind will grow in a safe and clear environment. You are free
to accept, reflect and critique these messages of the Frog Spirit. That is actually what
the Frog Spirit wants from you - to think on your own and follow your intuition.

The Frog Cult will mainly concentrate on the philosophical part, while
Limbo series will focus on the rhetorical aspects. Together, they will
assist everyone to achieve enlightenment and spread the ideas to others.
